# Team : The Ching Dynasty
## Game : Drink N Drive
###Team members : Ayush Singh, Sakshi Priyam, Pranesh S


## Controls
• Accelerate : W, Up Arrow    or Right Stick UP
• Decelerate : S,  Down Arrow or Right Stick Down
• Brakes     : Spacebar       or any Trigger
• Steer      : A-D, Left-Right Arrow Keys   or Left Stick
• Drink      : E              or any Shoulder Button
• Pause      : Escape


## Gameplay
• The game is essentially a race among drunk drivers. Drinking gives you a 20%(stacking) speed boost each time but each drink affects your vision. The vision effects are intentionally designed to make you feel dizzy so play with caution.
• Use accelerate to move forward, decelerate to move backward and brakes for stopping any motion.
• You cannot spam the drinks. wait a couple of seconds between drinks.
• The theme selected is "The More You Use It, The Worse It Gets" and it is implemented as `The more you Drink, the worse your Vision gets`.


## Some Features
• The enemy cars are also drunk so if provoked by driving in front of them, you may force them to crash into the trees. But without provoking they are fearless drivers and will give tough competition.
• There are 5 hidden cows in the map which can be killed(I do not suggest this).
• The gameplay is smoother when played on a gamepad.
• There may or may not be hidden shortcuts among the trees.
